Name Picker

Program Description:
The Name Picker serves as a tool for teachers to randomly select students from specific classes to participate in activities such as answering questions or demonstrating knowledge. The program ensures that each student in the class gets an equal opportunity to be called on before any student is selected again. Through a graphical user interface (GUI) built using the Tkinter library, the teacher can select a class period and then randomly pick a student's name from that class. This feature promotes fairness and engagement in classroom interactions by providing an unbiased method for selecting participants.

Agar.io

Program Description:
The objective is to grow the cell by consuming smaller cells and pellets while avoiding being consumed by larger cells. Player control his/her cell using mouse movements, directing it to move around the playing field. As cells consume pellets or smaller cells, they grow in size, making them more formidable and allowing them to consume even larger cells. However, larger cells move slower, making them more vulnerable to smaller, faster cells.

Space Invaders

Program Description:
Space Invaders is a retro-inspired arcade game where players assume control of a spaceship tasked with defending Earth from invading alien forces. Players maneuver their spaceship horizontally along the bottom of the screen, aiming to shoot down descending waves of enemy aliens before they reach the planet's surface. With each successful elimination, players earn points and progress through increasingly challenging levels. Along the way, they must strategically dodge enemy fire while utilizing power-ups and special weapons to turn the tide of battle in their favor. Can you save Earth from the alien invasion and become the ultimate space defender? Play Space Invaders to find out!

Snake

Program Description:
Snake is a classic arcade-style game where players control a snake navigating through a grid-based environment. Starting as a small snake, players must maneuver around the grid, eating food to grow longer while avoiding collisions with the walls or the snake's own tail. As the snake grows, the game becomes progressively more challenging, requiring quick reflexes and strategic planning to continue growing without running into obstacles.
